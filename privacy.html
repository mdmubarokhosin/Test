Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

ShighroPay collects information to provide better services to our users. The types of information we collect include:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and billing address
  • Transaction Data: Payment amounts, methods, dates, and transaction references
  • Device Information: Browser type, device type, IP address, and operating system
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, and interaction patterns

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Processing and completing payment transactions
  • Providing customer support and resolving disputes
  • Sending transaction notifications and receipts
  • Improving our services and user experience
  • Detecting and preventing fraud or unauthorized activities
  • Complying with legal obligations and regulations

3. Data Storage & Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Data is encrypted in transit using TLS/SSL
  • Firebase Realtime Database with security rules
  • Regular security audits and monitoring
  • Access controls and authentication mechanisms
  • Data backup and disaster recovery procedures

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Payment processors (bKash, Nagad, Rocket, Upay, SureCash) to complete transactions
  • Service providers who assist in operating our platform
  • Law enforcement when required by law or legal process
  • Third parties with your explicit consent

5. Cookies & Tracking

We use local storage and cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences (dark mode, language)
  • Maintain your session state
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve our service

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data stored by us
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Export your transaction data

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. Transaction data is retained for a minimum of 5 years for compliance and audit purposes.

8. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy regularly.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
