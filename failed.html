Frequently Asked Questions

Why did my payment fail?

Payments can fail due to insufficient balance, network issues, incorrect credentials, or daily transaction limits being reached.

Will I be charged for a failed payment?

No. Failed payments are not processed and no amount is deducted. If you see a deduction, it will be auto-reversed within 24-72 hours.
