Frequently Asked Questions

How do I make a payment?

Visit our payment page, fill in your details, select your preferred payment method, and follow the prompts to complete your payment.

What payment methods are supported?

We support bKash, Nagad, Rocket, Upay, and SureCash mobile financial services.

How long do refunds take?

Refunds are typically processed within 5-10 business days depending on the payment method used. You will receive a notification once the refund has been processed.

Yes, we use industry-standard SSL encryption to protect all data transmitted through our platform. Read our Privacy Policy for more details.
